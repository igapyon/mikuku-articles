# Image Generation Prompt

Create a wide horizontal graphic recording style educational poster for a Japanese technical article titled:

「Mermaid から入る UML 入門」

The poster explains how readers can start from Mermaid diagrams in Markdown and then understand nearby UML diagram types. It should feel like a clean Note.com article title image and a hand-drawn whiteboard explainer.

## Layout

Wide poster composition, 16:9.

Left and center: a large hand-drawn graphic recording board.

Right side: Mikuku explains the board. Mikuku is head-and-shoulders or upper body, looking toward the left-side diagrams. Do not let Mikuku hold any objects. Hands can be relaxed and simple, partly outside frame if needed.

## Visual Story

Show a clear flow:

Markdown document
→ Mermaid text diagrams
→ visual diagrams
→ UML diagram names
→ design conversation becomes easier

Use arrows, boxes, small icons, sticky-note-like labels, and hand-drawn connectors.

## Style

Japanese anime-style technical explainer illustration.
Graphic recording illustration, hand-drawn infographic poster, cozy whiteboard notes, warm clean article visual.
Readable-looking Japanese labels, not too tiny.
Soft warm paper background, restrained beige base with accent colors: teal, coral, warm yellow, soft green, dark brown ink.
Thin clean line art, soft watercolor-like shading, friendly but not childish.
High information density, clear hierarchy, good margins.

## Avoid

Do not make a corporate slide deck.
Do not make the image dark, futuristic, or photo-realistic.
Do not redesign Mikuku.
Do not let Mikuku hold a pen, pointer, marker, notebook, board, or any object.
Avoid distorted hands or extra fingers.
Avoid unreadably dense walls of text.
Avoid claiming Mermaid and UML are exactly the same.
