# Image Generation Prompt

Create one wide horizontal graphic recording poster for a Japanese technical article.

Aspect ratio: wide landscape, 16:9. High-resolution educational infographic poster. Japanese anime-style technical explainer, hand-drawn graphic recording, warm clean Note.com article visual, soft bright light, readable composition, cozy whiteboard and notebook feeling.

## Canonical Mikuku Character Prompt

Use this as the canonical character prompt for Mikuku. Same character, do not redesign, preserve character identity, canonical character reference.

masterpiece, best quality, ultra-detailed anime portrait, head-and-shoulders 1girl, shy uneasy expression, slight 3/4 view, looking to her right and slightly upward.

Warm chestnut-brown eyes with gold flecks, soft blush, worried upward-slanted brows, small downturned pout, fair warm skin, delicate V-U shaped chin.

Soft caramel-chestnut very long twin-tails tied high with black ribbons and deep-red trim, silky wavy hair, asymmetrical curtain bangs, long side strands framing cheeks.

Thin clean line art, soft cel shading, watercolor-like shading, subtle rim light, warm minimal background.

For this poster, place Mikuku on the right side, half body or bust-up, facing and looking toward the diagrams on the left. She has a gentle shy teaching expression. Do not let Mikuku hold any objects. Hands may be relaxed and simple, partly hidden by sleeves or outside the frame.

## Main Poster Theme

Title text in Japanese, large and clear:

「生成AIの MCP は、妖精さんへお願いする魔法陣」

Subtitle:

「会話の外へ、小さなお願いをちゃんと届ける形」

## Layout

Left and center: dense hand-drawn graphic recording map.
Right: Mikuku explaining with one speech bubble.
Background: warm off-white paper, not dark, not sci-fi, not corporate slide style.
Use soft brown, charcoal, muted teal, gentle red accents, pale yellow highlights. Avoid a one-color palette.

## Required Diagram Content

Draw a central glowing-but-soft magic circle labeled:

「MCP = 魔法陣」

From top-left to center to bottom-left, show this flow with arrows:

「ユーザー」→「生成AI agent」→「MCP」→「妖精さん」→「結果が戻る」

Add a small note:

「魔法陣は仕事をする相手ではなく、お願いを届ける通り道」

Around the MCP magic circle, create five labeled boxes:

1. 「MCP server」=「受付・場」
2. 「tool」=「お願いできるお仕事」
3. 「resource」=「読みに行ける資料」
4. 「tool schema」=「お願いの術式」
5. 「client / agent」=「お願いを出す側」

Draw exactly four small cute rabbit-like fairy mascots, simple and symbolic, not realistic. The mapping must follow the article exactly. Do not swap these mappings. Do not draw unrelated white rabbits in the technical term boxes.

- 「CLI」: 明るい茶色のネザーランドドワーフ風。small bright light-brown Netherland Dwarf rabbit fairy, compact upright ears, near a terminal icon, local and energetic.
- 「API」: 白と淡いグレーのホーランドロップ風。white and pale-gray Holland Lop rabbit fairy, soft drooping lop ears, near a cloud doorway or remote entrance, calm receptionist feeling.
- 「database」: 黒に近いチョコレート色のミニレッキス風。near-black chocolate Mini Rex rabbit fairy, sleek quiet guide, near records or stacked disks.
- 「MCP server internal function」: クリーム色のジャージーウーリー風。cream Jersey Wooly rabbit fairy, fluffy woolly appearance, inside the MCP server area, small internal worker.

Important mapping rule: technical concept boxes such as 「tool」, 「resource」, 「tool schema」, and 「client / agent」 may have icons, scrolls, books, or robots, but they must not introduce extra rabbit fairies that confuse the four article mappings. If a rabbit appears in a box, it must match one of the four mappings above and be labeled accordingly.

Use small labels only; text should look hand-written but readable.

## Visual Quality Rules

Make it a graphic recording illustration, not a UI screen. Use arrows, boxes, icons, simple magic circle motifs, notebook labels, and warm educational visual hierarchy. Keep text short and readable. Do not include random unreadable paragraphs. Do not make Mikuku hold a pen, pointer, board, notebook, or any object. Avoid extra fingers, malformed hands, duplicate arms, or character redesign.
