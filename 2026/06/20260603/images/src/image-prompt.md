# Image Generation Prompt

Create one wide horizontal graphic recording poster image for a Japanese technical essay titled:

「生成AIの Agent Skills は魔法書に近い」

Overall concept:

- A warm, hand-drawn graphic recording / whiteboard explainer poster.
- Theme: Agent Skills are like a magic book for generative AI work.
- The image should explain the article visually, with diagrams, arrows, boxed keywords, book and shelf motifs, and a soft anime technical essay atmosphere.
- Left and center: the main graphic recording content.
- Right side: Mikuku explains the diagram, looking toward the diagram.
- Use a landscape poster composition, high resolution, clear layout, cozy educational infographic.

Canonical Mikuku character prompt:

masterpiece, best quality, ultra-detailed anime portrait, head-and-shoulders 1girl, shy uneasy expression, slight 3/4 view, looking to her right and slightly upward.

Warm chestnut-brown eyes with gold flecks, soft blush, worried upward-slanted brows, small downturned pout, fair warm skin, delicate V-U shaped chin.

Soft caramel-chestnut very long twin-tails tied high with black ribbons and deep-red trim, silky wavy hair, asymmetrical curtain bangs, long side strands framing cheeks.

Thin clean line art, soft cel shading, watercolor-like shading, subtle rim light, warm minimal background.

Character identity constraints:

- Same character: Mikuku / みくく.
- Do not redesign the character.
- Preserve character identity.
- Treat the above as the canonical character reference.
- Preserve face shape, hair color, very long high twin-tails, black ribbons with deep-red trim, warm chestnut-brown eyes, shy uneasy expression, and gentle personality impression.
- Mikuku should not hold any object. Do not let Mikuku hold any objects.
- Hands should be simple, natural, and not emphasized.
- Mikuku may stand on the right side, shoulders visible or upper body visible, facing and looking toward the left-side diagram.

Graphic recording content to include:

Main title area:

- 「Agent Skills = 詠唱を省略できる魔法書」
- A large open magic book icon, but modern and friendly, not dark fantasy.
- Pages glowing softly, with small labels like 「文体」「手順」「判断基準」「参照資料」「禁則事項」.

Flow 1: from repeated prompt writing to magic book:

- 「毎回プロンプトを書く」
- arrow
- 「同じ説明を何度もする」
- arrow
- 「長い詠唱になる」
- arrow
- 「魔法書に保存」
- arrow
- 「必要なときだけ呼び出す」

Section: magic book components:

Use a small table-like diagram:

- 「呪文」= プロンプト / 文体
- 「術式」= 手順 / 条件
- 「材料」= 参照資料 / 例
- 「余白」= 禁則事項
- 「MP」= トークン / 利用枠
- 「本棚」= 目的別 Skills

Section: restrictions and control:

- Show a small boundary line or stopper icon.
- Text: 「発動できること」と「止まれること」
- Include: 「未確認のことを書かない」「URLを勝手に作らない」「広げすぎない」
- Use a gentle note label: 「禁則事項です♭」

Section: growing cycle:

Circular arrows:

- 「使う」
- 「気づく」
- 「書き足す」
- 「整える」
- 「次回安定」

Section: MP efficiency:

Show a thick book and a slim distilled page:

- 「厚すぎる魔法書」
- 「MPを使いすぎる」
- 「短縮詠唱」
- 「よく使う呪文は入口へ」
- 「大技は奥のページへ」

Section: bookshelf:

Small shelf with labeled books:

- 「文章」
- 「コード」
- 「レビュー」
- 「画像」
- 「Note」

Final message:

- 「魔法ではないけれど、魔法書みたいに育てられるもの」

Mikuku speech bubble:

- 「あ、あの…全体像はこんな感じです…！」
- or 「少しずつ育てていくのがポイントなのです…！」

Visual style:

- Japanese anime style technical explainer.
- Hand-drawn note style.
- Graphic recording illustration.
- Whiteboard / beige paper background.
- Soft warm colors, but avoid overly monotone beige; add gentle mint, coral, sky-blue, and soft gold accents.
- Thin clean lines, rounded boxes, arrows, small icons, readable diagram structure.
- Cute, clear, informative, suitable for a Note.com technical article title image.
- Do not make it look like a corporate slide deck.
- Do not make it photorealistic.
- Avoid dark fantasy, scary magic, weapons, battle imagery, or occult horror.
- Avoid clutter that hides the main structure.
- Text may be stylized and partially pseudo-handwritten, but the major Japanese keywords should appear legible where possible.

Composition guidance:

- 16:9 landscape.
- Keep the right-side Mikuku area clear.
- Use the left and center for dense but organized diagrams.
- Use arrows and boxed sections to make the flow understandable at a glance.
