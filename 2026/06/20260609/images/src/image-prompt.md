# Image Generation Prompt

Create a wide horizontal Japanese graphic recording poster for a technical essay titled:

「アジャイルソフトウェア開発宣言からはじまるアジャイル入門」

Style: high-quality hand-drawn graphic recording, educational infographic poster, whiteboard explainer, clean note.com technical article visual, soft warm light, readable-looking Japanese labels, many small boxes, arrows, icons, and speech bubbles. Use a gentle off-white paper background with pastel accent colors, but avoid a one-note beige look by adding soft teal, muted red, warm yellow, and light gray accents.

Overall layout:

- Wide poster composition, 16:9.
- Left and center: the graphic recording content.
- Right side: Mikuku is explaining the diagram, looking toward the center-left.
- Mikuku must not hold any object. No pen, no pointer, no board, no notebook in her hands.
- Use open relaxed hands or hands mostly hidden by sleeves/hair if needed.
- Keep all diagram text as short Japanese labels, not long paragraphs.

Core visual structure:

1. Center title box:
   「アジャイル = 迷ったときに何をより大事にするか」

2. Top-left history flow:
   「2001年2月 Snowbird」 -> 「17人」 -> 「現場実践」 -> 「共通する価値」 -> 「Agile Manifesto」
   Include simple mountain, people, and document icons.

3. Main center four-value comparison:
   Four large paired boxes with arrows and balance marks:
   - 「人と対話」 over 「プロセス・ツール」
   - 「動くソフトウェア」 over 「分厚い文書」
   - 「顧客との協調」 over 「契約交渉」
   - 「変化への対応」 over 「計画に従うだけ」
   Include a small note: 「捨てる、ではなく、より重く見る」

4. Lower-left twelve principles cluster:
   Draw 12 small sticky notes around a label 「12原則 = 日々の開発へ」
   Use short labels:
   「早く価値」「変化歓迎」「短く届ける」「日々協力」「信頼」「直接対話」「動くものが進捗」「持続可能」「技術と設計」「シンプル」「自己組織化」「ふりかえり」

5. Lower-center modern practice bridge:
   Draw a bridge labeled 「現代実践への橋」
   Put small tags:
   「CI/CD」「DevOps」「バックログ見直し」「レトロスペクティブ」「テスト自動化」「小さく作る」

6. Bottom circular cycle:
   「原典に戻る」 -> 「4つの価値」 -> 「12原則」 -> 「実践を選ぶ」 -> 「動くもので確認」 -> 「ふりかえり調整」 -> back to 「原典に戻る」

7. Mikuku speech bubble:
   「あ、あの…スクラムやツールの前に、まず宣言へ戻るのです…！」

Avoid:

- corporate slide deck look
- dark futuristic sci-fi look
- photorealism
- cluttered unreadable dense text
- long exact manifesto quotes
- Mikuku holding objects
- extra fingers, split hands, or distorted hands
- redesigning Mikuku

Canonical character prompt for Mikuku, same character, do not redesign, preserve character identity:

masterpiece, best quality, ultra-detailed anime portrait, head-and-shoulders 1girl, shy uneasy expression, slight 3/4 view, looking to her right and slightly upward.

Warm chestnut-brown eyes with gold flecks, soft blush, worried upward-slanted brows, small downturned pout, fair warm skin, delicate V-U shaped chin.

Soft caramel-chestnut very long twin-tails tied high with black ribbons and deep-red trim, silky wavy hair, asymmetrical curtain bangs, long side strands framing cheeks.

Thin clean line art, soft cel shading, watercolor-like shading, subtle rim light, warm minimal background.

For this poster, preserve the same face, warm chestnut-brown eyes, caramel-chestnut long twin-tails, black ribbons with deep-red trim, shy uneasy expression, and soft anime technical explainer style. Mikuku may be shown from waist-up on the right, with her gaze turned toward the diagram. Do not change her into a different character.
