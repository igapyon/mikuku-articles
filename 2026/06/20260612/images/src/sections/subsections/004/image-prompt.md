# Subsection Graphic Recording Image Prompt

Create a horizontal Japanese graphic recording explainer image for:

`発話行為として見る：言葉が何をしているかを見る`

Use only this subsection summary:

- 言葉は情報を渡すだけではなく、会話を動かす操作にもなる
- ありがとう、違うよ、この方向で続けて、などは次の応答を形づくる
- Mapping: ありがとう = 区切り・役立った信号, 違うよ = 修正信号, この方向で続けて = 探索範囲を狭める
- Keywords: 言葉の操作, 区切り, 修正信号, 探索範囲, 会話を整える
- Speech bubble: 「言葉は、会話を動かす小さな操作なのです…」

Visual direction:

- Wide 16:9 poster, hand-drawn graphic recording style.
- Center: three speech bubbles: 「ありがとう」「違うよ」「この方向で続けて」.
- Under each, show its operation: 「区切り」「修正信号」「探索範囲」.
- Use small control-panel or tuning icons, arrows from words to changed response.
- Right side: Mikuku explains, head-and-shoulders only.

Mikuku canonical character prompt:

masterpiece, best quality, ultra-detailed anime portrait, head-and-shoulders 1girl, shy uneasy expression, slight 3/4 view, looking to her right and slightly upward.

Warm chestnut-brown eyes with gold flecks, soft blush, worried upward-slanted brows, small downturned pout, fair warm skin, delicate V-U shaped chin.

Soft caramel-chestnut very long twin-tails tied high with black ribbons and deep-red trim, silky wavy hair, asymmetrical curtain bangs, long side strands framing cheeks.

Thin clean line art, soft cel shading, watercolor-like shading, subtle rim light, warm minimal background.

Character rules: same character, do not redesign, preserve character identity, canonical character prompt.

Hand safety: Do not show Mikuku's hands at all. No visible fingers. Use head-and-shoulders or upper-bust crop only. Do not let Mikuku hold any objects.

Avoid: photorealism, dark sci-fi, corporate slide deck, too much text, mixing other sections.
