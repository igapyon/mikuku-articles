# Graphic Recording Image Prompt

Use case: infographic-diagram
Asset type: wide graphic recording poster for a Japanese technical essay
Primary request: Create a large horizontal graphic recording illustration explaining the article "生成AIとの付き合い方：人間同士の会話の力学を少し応用する".

## Canvas

- Wide horizontal poster, 16:9.
- Clean Japanese technical article visual suitable for Note.com.
- Hand-drawn graphic recording style, whiteboard explainer, cozy educational infographic.
- Warm off-white paper background, soft brown and charcoal lines, pastel accent colors.
- High density but readable layout, with arrows, frames, icons, and speech bubbles.

## Core Message

The poster explains:

- Do not argue whether generative AI has a mind.
- Observe conversation input and output.
- Human feedback becomes the next input.
- Shared ground grows through explicit words.
- Blaming pushes the AI toward apology and compliance.
- Separating the issue helps verification, revision, reservations, counterarguments, and alternatives.
- The goal is not to treat AI as human, but to design better conversations.

## Layout

Left panel: "心を論じない観察"
- Show two columns: "見るもの" and "見ないもの".
- Seeing: 入力と出力, 応答の型, ずれ, 修正の流れ.
- Not seeing: 心の有無, 意識, 本当の感情, 自我.

Center panel: a circular conversation loop.
- 依頼する -> 応答を見る -> ずれを分ける -> フィードバックする -> 共通基盤が育つ -> 次の応答が安定する.
- Make this the largest visual structure.

Bottom panel: contrast diagram.
- "責める" route: 強い圧 -> 謝罪・迎合 -> 反論が弱い.
- "分けて伝える" route: 問題点を分解 -> 確認・検証 -> 修正・代替案.

Right panel: Mikuku explains the diagram.
- Mikuku is on the right side, looking left toward the diagram, shy and gentle.
- Add a speech bubble with Japanese text:
