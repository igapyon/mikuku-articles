# Image Generation Prompt

Create a single wide horizontal graphic recording illustration for a Japanese technical essay titled:

「生成AI時代のアジャイル：4つの価値に起きるパラダイムシフト」

## Composition

Wide 16:9 educational infographic poster, hand-drawn graphic recording style, clean whiteboard / warm paper note style.

Place the main graphic recording area on the left and center. Place Mikuku on the right side, explaining the diagram while looking toward the central content. Mikuku must not hold any objects.

Central large title:
「生成AI時代のアジャイル」

Central message:
「4つの価値は消えない。場所・速度・役割分担・判断軸が変わる」

## Main Diagram

Show the Agile Manifesto as a stable foundation at the bottom. From it, draw four branches or panels:

1. 「人と対話」
   - Before: 人と人が話す
   - After: 生成AIも対話の輪に加わる
   - Visuals: people, speech bubbles, an AI seat, notes

2. 「動くソフトウェア」
   - Before: 後で動くものを見る
   - After: 目の前で動くものを触りながら確認
   - Visuals: prototype screen, play icon, small app window, arrows

3. 「顧客との協調」
   - Before: 顧客が話し、専門家が具体化
   - After: 顧客・専門家・生成AIで意図、価値、コストを一緒に確認
   - Visuals: customer, expert, AI, cost meter

4. 「変化への対応」
   - Before: 変更コストが大きい
   - After: 変化を小さく分けて試す。AIが変える社会や業務にも対応
   - Visuals: split arrows, loop cycle, plan sheet, changing environment

Add a circular flow:
「対話 → 構造化 → すぐ試作 → 触って確認 → 小さく修正 → 次の対話」

Add a strong note near the center:
「人間の判断が中心」

Add a caution note:
「動いただけでは十分ではない。意味・安全性・運用・次につながるかを見る」

## Speech Bubble

Mikuku says:
「あ、あの…生成AIはアジャイルを置き換えず、4つの価値を近い距離で試させるのです…！」

## Style

Japanese anime-style technical explainer, graphic recording illustration, soft warm lighting, high detail, hand-drawn icons, arrows, boxes, sticky notes, readable-looking Japanese headings, cozy but professional, suitable for a Note.com technical article.

Use a balanced palette: warm paper base, charcoal line art, soft teal, coral, yellow, and muted blue accents. Do not make it dark, corporate, flat UI-like, photo-realistic, or overly sci-fi.

## Character Constraints

Mikuku is on the right side, visible from about waist-up or bust-up, softly smiling with a slightly worried gentle expression. She looks toward the infographic. She does not hold a pen, pointer, notebook, board, or any object. Hands may be relaxed near her chest or mostly hidden by framing to avoid hand/finger errors.

## Avoid

Avoid redesigned character, different hair color, missing twin-tails, missing ribbons, hard sci-fi armor, dark cyberpunk mood, corporate slide deck, excessive tiny unreadable text, photo-realism, object held by Mikuku, extra fingers, duplicated hands, distorted face.
