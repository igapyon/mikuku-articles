Create one wide horizontal Japanese graphic recording poster for a technical essay titled:

「GPT-5.5とQwen3-Thinkingでは、プロンプト / Agent Skills 像が違って見える」

## Overall Scene

A clean, warm, hand-drawn graphic recording poster. The right side shows Mikuku gently explaining the diagram. The left and center contain a structured whiteboard-style infographic about Agent Skills design across GPT-5.5 and Qwen3-Thinking.

Use a wide 16:9 poster composition. The visual should feel like a Japanese technical article explainer for Note.com: friendly, precise, high-information, readable-looking, with soft handwritten labels, arrows, boxes, and small icons.

1. Top layer: two model boxes side by side
   - Left: "GPT-5.5"

2. Middle layer: shared knowledge base
   - Large central box: "Common Content Agent Skills"

3. Lower layer: distillation and driving layer
   - Box: "Distillation Layer / meaning map"
   - Box: "Qwen-specific Driving Skill fork"

     Common Content -> Distillation Layer -> Qwen Driving Layer -> Stable Work

"Reasoning power and context operation ability are different things."

## Visual Style

- Japanese anime-style technical explainer
- hand-drawn graphic recording
- whiteboard and notebook feeling
- warm but not too beige-dominant
- soft cream background with teal, coral, muted green, charcoal, and small gold accents
- many arrows, boxes, sticky notes, small icons, markdown symbols, folders, books, and simple map icons
- high detail, balanced layout, no overlapping text blocks
- readable-looking Japanese labels, but do not rely on tiny text
- clean visual hierarchy

## Avoid

- photorealism
- corporate slide deck look
- dark sci-fi atmosphere
- excessive purple-blue gradient
- single-color palette
- Mikuku holding a pen, pointer, notebook, board, or any object
- distorted hands, extra fingers, or split hands
- changing Mikuku's face, hair color, twin-tails, ribbons, or eye style
