# Image Generation Prompt

Use case: infographic-diagram
Asset type: landscape graphic recording poster for a Japanese Note.com technical essay

Create a single high-resolution landscape graphic recording illustration poster. The article theme is: "Why can we use generative AI subscriptions at such approachable prices?" The poster should explain that today's AI subscription pricing may be a strategic runway period, while the real compute costs are large and pricing is gradually moving closer to usage-based reality.

Canvas and composition:
- Wide landscape poster, 16:9 or similar.
- Beige paper-like background, hand-drawn graphic recording style.
- Left and center: dense but readable visual explanation with boxes, arrows, small icons, and handwritten-style Japanese labels.
- Right side: Mikuku explains the poster, looking toward the central diagram.
- Mikuku must not hold any object.
- Keep all text short, diagram-like, and mostly Japanese.

Canonical character prompt for Mikuku, same character, do not redesign, preserve character identity:

masterpiece, best quality, ultra-detailed anime portrait, head-and-shoulders 1girl, shy uneasy expression, slight 3/4 view, looking to her right and slightly upward.

Warm chestnut-brown eyes with gold flecks, soft blush, worried upward-slanted brows, small downturned pout, fair warm skin, delicate V-U shaped chin.

Soft caramel-chestnut very long twin-tails tied high with black ribbons and deep-red trim, silky wavy hair, asymmetrical curtain bangs, long side strands framing cheeks.

Thin clean line art, soft cel shading, watercolor-like shading, subtle rim light, warm minimal background.

Mikuku character constraints:
- Same character Mikuku / みくく.
- Do not redesign her.
- Preserve face shape, eye style, hair style, hair color, twin tails, black ribbons with deep-red trim, shy and slightly worried personality impression.
- She may face left and look at the diagram.
- She should have a gentle Japanese anime tech-explainer look.
- No pen, pointer, marker, notebook, board, or handheld object.
- Hands may be relaxed near her chest or at her sides, with simple natural fingers.

Main poster title text:
「生成AIは、なぜ親しみやすい価格で使えるの？」

Visual style:
- Japanese graphic recording, hand-drawn whiteboard note, cozy educational tech explainer.
- Warm but not monochrome: beige base, soft brown line art, muted teal, coral, mustard, and gentle green accents.
- Cute, readable, informative, Note.com article friendly.
- Thin clean lines, soft watercolor-like fills, mild shadows, lots of whitespace around major boxes.
- Small icons should be simple and symbolic, not photorealistic.

Text quality constraints:
- Use short Japanese labels.
- Avoid dense paragraphs.
- Text should look like readable handwritten labels, but it is acceptable if some tiny secondary text is approximate.
- Do not add unrelated brand logos.
- Avoid dark cyberpunk, sterile corporate slides, photorealistic rendering, hard sci-fi atmosphere, excessive purple/blue gradients.

Negative constraints:
- No object in Mikuku's hands.
- No extra characters.
- No company logos.
- No scary or dark mood.
- No weapon-like objects.
- No distorted extra hands or six-finger hands.
